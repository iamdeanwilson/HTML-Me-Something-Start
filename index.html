The Story of Kai: A Very Good Boi

This is Kai

As you can see, Kai is a good boi, but you may be asking yourself, "What is it about Kai that makes him such a good boi?" This question has been plaguing researchers for years. It is obvious by looking at him that he is a good boi, there's no question there. But the exact reasons as to why he is such a good boi are often a mystery. Well, good news! We're here to decode the mystery of why Kai is such a good boi.

Humble Beginnings

'Twas a cold and snowy winter's eve, right around Christmas of 2016, when a pregnant dog named Sassy sought shelter from the frigid conditions to bring new life into the world. Sassy had no home, so she knew that in order to give her pups a chance at survival, she would need to find shelter. Luckily for her, she stumbled upon a small shed that provided just enough shelter to keep her warm, and within this shed, gave birth to a litter of pups. It wasn't easy, but she survived. The owner of the shed was surprised to discover Sassy with two tiny little puppies huddled for warmth. It is unknown how long they sat in that shed before they were found; it could have been hours, or it could have been days. Fortunately, the kind person brought the three surviving dogs inside to warm them up and feed them, but was unable to provide them with a forever home, which is how Kai ended up with his first foster mom, Edith, from Needy Paws Rescue. After surviving the harsh wintery conditions of his birth, Kai was ready to take on the world! After spending the first 3 months of his life with Edith, Kai was ready for his forever home, and that's when he jumped into Dean's life!

The Early Years

Unsurprisingly, Kai was a bit skiddish and anxious as a young pup. His first experience on this planet were a battle for survival, so he wasn't sure what was safe and what was dangerous. Always skeptical of new people, dogs, toys, places, and practically anything unfamiliar, Kai had a lot of learning to do. But he is a brave and smart boi. He's a survivor. He's got what it takes to make it in this world!

Coming Out of His Shell

Kai didn't always like new people. The first time he met Dean, he cowered in a corner on the opposite side of the room while Dean tried to bribe him with treats and toys. It took 45 minutes before Kai reluctantly slunk over to investigate the ball that Dean was holding. After realizing Dean wasn't a threat, it was time to play! It may have taken 45 minutes to break the ice, but once the ice was broken, he unleashed the energy he had been concealing, and wanted nothing more than to play, play, play! After a couple of weeks settling into his new home, Kai was becoming more confortable with his new environment. At first, he was very skeptical of these so-called "walks", and wouldn't leave the stoop. Eventually though, he learned that walks are the best, and now simply saying the word "walk" will send him into a frenzy of screeching excitement. It may have been a rough start, but Kai was starting to get pretty cozy in his new home.

Workin' Dog

When Kai first entered Dean's life, Dean was working at a rock climbing gym that allowed dogs in the facility, which meant Kai could go to work with Dean! Kai and Dean were attached at the hip; everywhere that Dean went, Kai joined. Kai was a people pleaser. He always sought to satisfy the people around him. He was a quick learner, and picked up a lot of fun tricks in a short amount of time. He loved to show off his tricks, but especially when a treat was on the line. When working at Upper Limits, he loved to run around and make new friends with all of the people in the gym.

Nature Boi

Kai LOVES being outside. Walking, Hiking, Camping, Swimming; If it's outside, he loves it! Kai's been camping in Utah, Swimming in Arkansas, hiking in Colorado, and many more adventures all around the USA; the list could go on and on!

Benji & Kai

In Spring of 2020, when much of the world was shut down due to the pandemic, Kai got a little brother! One of Kai's human roommates decided to adopt a puppy named Benji. They hit it off from day one and were the bestest of buds. They played all day and cuddled all night. Benji is a good boi in his own right, and an entire separate webpage could be created for Benji, but to keep things short, we'll just reiterate that Benji is a VERY good boi. However, in Spring of 2022, the human roommmates moved into different houses, and Kai and Benji were no longer housemates. However, even though they may not live together any longer, they are certainly still brothers and best friends forever! Benji and Kai still get to see each other multiple times a week, and their bond is as strong as ever.

Where is He Now?

In 2023, Kai is nearing 7 years old, but is just as happy as ever! He's slowing down a bit, and loves any opportunity to take a nap on a nice big pillow, but he can still get rowdy when he feels like it! He loves playing fetch, tug-o-war, and oddly enough, his favorite toy is just a simple cardboard paper towel roll. And the best part is that they're cheap and easily available! Kai has lots of friends, both human and dogs. He goes on walks almost every day, and travels with his human companions whenever and wherever he can!
